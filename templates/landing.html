How It Works

Four steps from upload to finished bundle

1

Select your court

Choose your country, jurisdiction, and court. The cover page is populated automatically with the correct court name and rules.

2

Upload your documents

Drag and drop PDFs or images into the individual documents area, or create tab groups to organize by category.

3

Arrange and name

Drag to reorder, click to rename any document. Preview the structure before you generate.

4

Download your bundle

Click Generate and download a professionally formatted PDF bundle complete with cover page, indexed table of contents and items hyperlinked directly to their pages.
